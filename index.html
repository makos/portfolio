About

I am a 22-years old self-taught hobbyist programmer, tinkering with computers since I can remember, and studying programming since I was 17. I'm working towards widening my field of expertise, to eventually earn a living doing what I love - working with computer systems. Be it coding web pages, console applications, games or managing operating systems, I enjoy any task that requires creative thinking and problem-solving skills.

Programming languages, frameworks and systems I'm experienced in:

  • Python
  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • jQuery, Bootstrap
  • shell scripting
  • GNU/Linux, various different distributions and versions
  • Windows

To relax I like to play video games and listen to music. I prefer games with interesting and fresh gameplay ideas, that don't necessarily feature amazing graphics. I like roguelikes, RTS games, simulations and an occasional FPS or arcade platformer.

As for music, I enjoy a wide variety of genres - from metal, hard rock, various soundtracks, to intelligent dance music (IDM), various other electronic genres and bits of classical and jazz music.
